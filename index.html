Work Summary

I followed the provided tutorial to create a base solar system animation with planets revolving around the Sun. Then, I extended the project with several enhancements:

  • Textures: Applied realistic textures for all planets.
  • Skymap: Added a starfield skymap background to increase realism and depth.
  • Lighting: Configured the Sun as a glowing emissive source, with proper light falloff to illuminate planets.
  • Orbits: Adjusted orbit speeds and rotations for each planet, scaled for visibility and proportionality.
  • Camera Shots: Created multiple cinematic camera shots and used the Camera Sequencer for smooth transitions.
