Storyboard & Description

The animation is a short Rube Goldberg sequence showing a chain reaction using six objects. A skateboard releases a ball that knocks over dominoes, which hit a fan. The fan spins and pushes an office chair, causing a ruler to flick and launch another ball into a trash can, completing the chain.

Objects in the Machine

Five required objects (plus one extra):

Skateboard: starting ramp releasing the ball.

Dominoes: transfer energy down the chain.

Fan: spins and propels the next object forward.

Office Chair: rolls from the fan’s force to trigger the ruler.
