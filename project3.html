Course: Computer Science 417 – Computer Animation

Storyboard & Description

8-beat storyboard (rough):

  • 1 – Falling: Ball drops from a building.
  • 2 – Bouncing: First ground impact and rebound.
  • 3 – Rolling: Rolls from sidewalk into the street.
  • 4 – Car Hit (Anticipation → Impact): Car strikes the ball.
  • 5 – Building Hit: Ball ricochets off another building.
  • 6 – Launch to Hoop: Ball arcs toward the rim.
  • 7 – Roll on Rim: Follows the rim’s curve, wobbling.
  • 8 – Drop In: Falls cleanly through the hoop.

Open storyboard PDF in a new tab

Animation Principles Used

  • Squash & Stretch — impact deformation on bounce.
  • Anticipation — pre-moment before the car strike.
  • Arc & Follow Through — ball rolling around the rim; overlapping action.
